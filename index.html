Who is this program for?

This program is made for B2B innovators reshaping the healthcare industry with the use of intelligent technologies for more efficient, safe, and accessible healthcare services for everyone. If you are one - this program is for you.

  • EHR companies

    Companies providing EHR systems and patient portals can use Infermedica technologies to extend their systems with an AI-driven initial patient data collection, triage direction, and preliminary diagnosis. This can result in:

    • engaging patients in a data collections proces
    • supporting medical staff with up-to-date patient data
    • providing physiciant with clinical decisions support
  • Telemedicine & virtual care providers

    Providers looking for a way to improve the flow of patients entering the virtual care systems with engaging, intuitive, and personalized tools can use Infermedica technologies to:

    • provide better patient management
    • improve patient navigation to virtual care services
    • gather initial patient data prior e-visits
  • IT system integrators

    Companies specialized in building complex end-to-end health platforms can use Infermedica solutions and expertise to complement their systems with flexible and easy-to-implement AI modules for:

    • data collection
    • preliminary diagnosis and triage
    • patient navigation
  • Employee benefits brokers & consultancies

    Organizations intending to enhance any basic healthcare services with self-diagnosis, triage suggestions, and specialist recommendation tools for patients and their families, can use Infermedica to:

    • implement new features in a short time
    • improve employee access to medical care
    • reduce the cost of care for employers
  • Healthcare chatbot providers

    Providers planning to develop chatbots and other conversational tools, dedicated to patient pre-diagnosis and triage, can use Infermedica's tools to:

    • enhance their solutions with NLP capacities
    • provide trusted pre-diagnostic scenarios
    • create unique and personalized experiences
